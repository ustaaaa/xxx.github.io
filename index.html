TECHNOLOGY & INNOVATION

Technology at Zeustone

The physical world and the financial world are both highly non-linear and dynamic, but we believe that we can understand them. Instead of using the conventional model-driven approaches relying on probably unrealistic assumptions, we turn to data-driven methods, and exploit the most recent advances in machine learning and high-performance computing, which understand the model directly from real data.

Our engineers have built modern cognitive systems that perform data aggregation from enormous sources including but not limited to the real-time financial market, global political and economic events, as well as the social medias. Our deep neural networks for market indicator extraction, black-swan event detection, market sentiment judgment, are used by our economists and trading teams for trading-decision making and risk control.

Innovation at Zeustone

We are not satisfied by only using adopt the existing machine learning methods. Instead, we invent new methodologies especially for asset management. This is achieved by defining new optimization criterion, regularization functions for the neural networks, and by integrating theories in economics into such design. This is helpful, for instance, to risk management since we are able to design a system that meets the risk tolerance for each individual investor.

The patterns in the real world change rapidly and our system is able to learn them in a self-contained manner. The evolution of the market automatically provides new labels that are prerequisite for supervised model updating; fast and stable algorithms are adopted to retrain the model parameters. We utilize transfer learning to solve the low-resource data problem in many assets, and also use semi-supervised learning such as teacher-student models to save the high cost of data tagging in many problems.
