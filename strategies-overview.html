Why Commodity Markets?
  • Huge market Potential: Active Trading and Adequate Liquidity

    • Commodities play a leading role in international trade, deeply influencing global economic growth. The commodity market is one of the most active trading markets in financial markets, with a huge market breadth and depth. As China's financial market becomes more mature, the market of commodity futures has developed rapidly. Therefore, we see a huge potential of commodity markets.

  • The approaching era of commodities under Sino-US tensions

    • With the escalation of global trade frictions, we see an increasing volatility of financial asset prices, so that the hedging characteristics of commodity trading become increasingly important. The link between commodity prices and interest rates, foreign exchange, stocks and other markets has strengthened, and macroeconomic factors are responded rapidly. Diverse strategies can be applied in the commodity market, which leads to excess returns in such a volatile market.
